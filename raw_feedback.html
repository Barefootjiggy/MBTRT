How much energy we have is impacted by many things, but mainly:

- Sleep
- What, why, and how we eat
- Exercise
- How much water we have

And

- Stress (Having a lot on our mind can make it feel like you're pulling a lead balloon).

The tricky part is: we need energy to do some of these things, but many of these things give us energy too!
